This site prices options at current day based on a Monte Carlo simulation

We simulate price paths using the following discretized Geometric Brownian Motion formula:

$$ S_{t+\Delta t} = S_t \cdot \exp\left[\left(\mu - \frac{1}{2} \sigma^2\right)\Delta t + \sigma \sqrt{\Delta t} \cdot Z\right] $$
